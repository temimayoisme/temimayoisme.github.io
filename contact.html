Temitayo is a narrative and commercial Film Director, Cinematographer always looking for the story. He’s created spots and branded content for clients such as Universal Music Group, Corona, Nigerian Consulate Shanghai, Burton, Temple of Light, San Oaks, etc. Before becoming a full-time director in 2019, he worked as an Associate Consultant (Marketing) and Creative Director at a fortune 500 company. When he’s not behind a camera, he’s also a PhD candidate, Jedi and Podcaster. Please feel free to reach out and send an email.
